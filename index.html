INFINITY DUEL

CONTROLS / TIPS

Movement

A / D or ← / → - Move left and right.

W or ↑ - Jump.

Q - Dash. You have dash charges and they recharge over time.

Combat

M1/Left Click - Normal Attack

Space - To guard, for pure defense

Perfect Parry - Press guard right before a hit. It heals a little, stuns nearby enemies, gains score, and creates a counter opening.

Form Tips - 1st Form is a single slash mainly for damage. 2nd Form has constant slashes and is capable of deflecting projectiles. 3rd Form provides pure defense and 4th being capable of defense and offense.

Skills

NOTE: To unleash a skill/form, click their key first then click M1.

G - Focus breathing. Breath can reach 180 now.

F - Total Concentration Breathing. Costs HP but it grants you auto-breathing.

1 - First Form: Surface Slash

2 - Second Form: Water Wheel. Can deflect projectiles, but physical attacks can still hit you.

3 - Third Form: Whirlpool. Defensive water form that protects you from any forms of attacks.

4 - Fourth Form: Flow Dance. Requires 140 breath. Final water form for offense and defense.

5 - Sun Breathing 1st Form: SUN DANCE. Requires 180 breath, costs HP, huge cooldown, high damage and protects you from any forms of attacks while it executes.

Arcade Progression

15K - Sniper Demon appears. Your max HP cap increases.

30K - Swift Demon appears. Guard and punish after dash attacks.

60K - Brute Demon appears. Use forms, not only M1.

70K - End of the milestone path. Akaza arrives, max HP cap becomes 485, and beating him ends the run.
