INFINITY DUEL

CONTROLS / TIPS

Movement

A / D or ← / → - Move left and right.

W or ↑ - Jump.

Q - Dash. You have dash charges and they recharge over time.

Combat

Left Click - Normal Attack

Space - To guard, for pure defense

Perfect Parry - Press guard right before a hit. It heals a little, stuns nearby enemies, gain score, and creates a counter opening

Skills

G - Focus breathing

1 - First Form: Surface Slash

2 - Second Form: Water Wheel

3 - Third Form: Whirlpool

4 - Fourth Form: Flow Dance

5 - Total Concentration Breathing. Costs your HP but it grants you auto-breathing

Arcade Progression

15K - Sniper Demon appears. Your max HP cap increases.

30K - Swift Demon appears. Guard and punish after dash attacks.

60K - Brute Demon appears. Use forms, not only M1.

70K - Boss warning. Akaza arrives, max HP cap becomes 485.
